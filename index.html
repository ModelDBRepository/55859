LRd VENTRICULAR CELL MODEL (GUINEA-PIG-TYPE)

The LRd model provides an accurate representation of the mammalian cardiac action potential and its underlying processes. Each component in the model was validated separately by reproducing physiological phenomena that involve a particular component. Individual processes were then combined into a complete model of the action potential. The LRd model has been used extensively in our lab and in others to uncover mechanistic explanations for physiologic and pathophysiologic phenomena in the single cell and in tissue preparations.
